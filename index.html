Projects

Comparison of the Demographic and Boba Stores of Seattle

Our group chose to research the ties between the opening dates of boba tea shops and the demographic changes in Seattle because we want to explore the ties between factors such as age, race, and sex and the demand of boba tea. Although boba tea is known to be widely appreciated by those who identify as Asian, we believe that it is also important to assess a variety of demographics to fully understand the significance of this food in its community and on the identities of those within it. Our project demonstrates how the environment and demographic surrounding a restaurant/shop that comes from and historically caters to one demographic can be affected by who its immediate audience is and how that audience changes over time.

WeFit - Plan a workout with friends

During the age of covid, college students have struggled to find time and opportunities to stay active. By interviewing college students and breaking down exercise applications already on the market, we are able to make the assumptions that fitness is about balance and access and proximity is important in determining how people want to exercise. That's why WeFit tackles both finding the right time and motivating students to exercise by planning fun workouts with your peers.
